About Us

ADZ Construction Co is a USA-based company founded by Mr. Andre Ferreira, dedicated to addressing the critical need for affordable housing in the United States. ADZ will start operations in 2025 in Florida, California, and Texas, with mid-term expansion plans to Ohio, Massachusetts, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, and New Jersey.

Our journey began in 2010 when ADZ's Brazilian branch was established. Since then, it has constructed over 400,000 sq ft of houses, apartments, commercial complexes, and industrial buildings.

Our team combines expertise in construction, real estate development, and investment support to deliver high-quality housing solutions that meet the needs of our communities, always at fair and affordable prices.

ADZ stands for Andre, Denise (Andre's wife and partner), and Zealous, reflecting our great enthusiasm, dedication, and passion for housing construction.

Our Services

At ADZ Construction Company & Development, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients:

Land Development

We specialize in developing affordable housing projects that are both sustainable and high-quality. Our projects are designed to meet the needs of modern living, with a focus on durability, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.

Rental Apartments

We offer a wide range of apartment rental options to suit different budgets and preferences. Our properties are well-maintained and located in desirable areas, providing comfortable and convenient living spaces for our tenants.

Investor Support

We provide comprehensive support to investors interested in the affordable housing sector. From market analysis and project planning to construction management and property management, we offer end-to-end services to ensure successful investments.
